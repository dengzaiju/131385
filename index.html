About The Game

Illumination of Mansion is an immersive puzzle adventure that challenges players to navigate through a mysterious mansion where light and shadow play crucial roles in progression. Set in a beautifully rendered Victorian-era mansion, players must solve intricate puzzles by manipulating light sources to reveal hidden pathways and clues.

The game combines elements of exploration, puzzle-solving, and atmospheric storytelling to create a captivating experience that will keep you engaged for hours.
